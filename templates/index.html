GuardFrame 1.0

GuardFrame is a simple tool for encrypting and decrypting text using the Caesar cipher. Enter your text, choose to encrypt or decrypt, set your key, and get instant results. Perfect for securing your messages with ease.

About GuardFrame

GuardFrame was created by Lakshan Induwara, an undergraduate at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura. As a passionate developer with a keen interest in cryptography and web development, Lakshan is dedicated to providing user-friendly tools that enhance digital privacy and security.

This is version 1.0 of GuardFrame, designed to offer a simple and effective solution for text encryption and decryption.
